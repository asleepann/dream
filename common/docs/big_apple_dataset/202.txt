Optis Wireless Technology, LLC and related entities (“Optis”) filed a lawsuit in the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Texas against the Company alleging that certain of the Company’s products infringe on patents owned by Optis.On August 11, 2020, a jury returned a verdict against the Company and awarded damages.In post-trial proceedings, the damages portion of the verdict was set aside.

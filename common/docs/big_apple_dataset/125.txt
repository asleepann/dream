Deemed Repatriation Tax Payable
As of September 25, 2021, the balance of the deemed repatriation tax payable imposed by the U.S. Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017 (the “Act”) was $24.6 billion, none of which is payable within 12 months.In addition to its cash requirements, the Company has a capital return program authorized by the Board of Directors.The Program does not obligate the Company to acquire any specific number of shares.As of September 25, 2021, the Company’s quarterly cash dividend was $0.22 per share.The Company intends to increase its dividend on an annual basis, subject to declaration by the Board of Directors.
